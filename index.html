I was postdoctoral scholar at Process Control group at Department of Chemical Engineering at NTNU, Trondheim. My research interests include numerical optimization algorithm, systems and control, embedded systems design, and software development. I am interested in applying efficient numerical methods for systems and control applications.

I obtained my PhD degree from Department of Engineering Cybernetics NTNU in 2012. My PhD project was on gradient-based optimization methods for large-scale system. I got my master and bachelor degrees from Departement of Electrical Engineering at Bandung Institute of Technology in 2002 and 2004, respectively. In between my academia years, I worked as IT consultant for SAP projects and embedded software developer for subsea control systems (mostly developing data communication protocol stacks of subsea sensors).

In my spare time, I enjoy being in nature, doing volunteer activities and contemplative practices.
